About

Kamiyama Marugoto Kosen is a private five-year college of technology located in Kamiyama, Tokushima, Japan.

Mission

The school invites students to study technology, design, and entrepreneurship as a single, integrated practice — and to grow into people who can shape their own future with their own hands.
