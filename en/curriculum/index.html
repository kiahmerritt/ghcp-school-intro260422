Curriculum

Technology, design, and entrepreneurship — three pillars studied as one across five years.

Technology

Programming, hardware, mathematics, and the sciences are built up carefully so that students can confidently work with the techniques that making things requires.

For course details, please refer to the official site ([to be confirmed]).

Design

From the basics of drawing and graphics to user experience and information design — students learn to think with both their hands and their heads, and to give form to their ideas.

Entrepreneurship

Students raise their own questions, work with peers, and bring the result out into the world. Through projects, they cultivate an entrepreneurial way of being.

A five-year arc

The earlier years emphasise foundations, while the later years build up specialisation and applied projects ([to be confirmed]). Please consult the official site for the current syllabus by year.
