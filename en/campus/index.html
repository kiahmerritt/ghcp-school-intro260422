Campus

Living in the town, learning from the town.

The school is fully residential. Students spend five years between the school building and the dormitory in the small town of Kamiyama.

Boarding

Residential life

From year one to year five, every student lives in the dormitory. Daily life itself is part of the curriculum — a setting in which autonomy and collaboration are practiced.

Meals are taken in the school cafeteria, with menus that incorporate local ingredients. [to be confirmed]

Town

The town of Kamiyama

Kamiyama is a town of roughly 4,500 people in the central part of Tokushima Prefecture. Surrounded by mountains and rivers, it has long attracted migrants, creators, and companies operating satellite offices.

The campus sits within the daily life of the town, and students naturally interact with local residents as part of their learning.
